The 208 Venture Challenge

Build. Compete. Lead.

A full-day, project-based competition to inaugurate the new Robotics & Entrepreneurship Clubs. This is your chance to build real-world skills, create a tangible project for your profile, and earn a leadership position.

The Four Challenges

Choose any two competitions to showcase your skills.

How Scoring Works

  • You will compete in **Duos (teams of 2)** for each of your two chosen events.
  • Each duo's project will receive a single score from the judges.
  • This score is then awarded to **both members** of the duo individually. For example, if your duo scores 90 points, you both get 90 points for that event.
  • Your final, individual rank is determined by the **sum of your scores** from both competitions you participated in.
  • The grand prize is awarded to the single student with the highest total score.

The Business Pitch

Develop a 5-slide pitch deck for a new venture that solves a problem on campus. The ultimate test of your entrepreneurial spirit.

The Photo-Caption

Capture 5 photos around campus that tell a cohesive story on a given theme. A challenge of creativity and visual storytelling.

Website Development

After a crash course in HTML/CSS, build and deploy a live profile website. The winning duo gets a premium domain name for a year.

The "Brain Bout" Quiz

A high-speed, multi-round quiz on logic, current events, and pop culture. Test your general knowledge under pressure.

Prizes & The Path to Leadership

The grand prize, podium positions, and club leadership roles are all awarded based on your **individual total score** from both competitions.
